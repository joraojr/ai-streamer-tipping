Phase 2: Tipping Phase

Instructions

After the Streaming Phase, we enter the Tipping Phase. In the Tipping Phase, the Viewers decide how much they would like to tip the Streamer.

Streamer

The Streamer waits while the Viewers decide on how much to tip.

Viewer

Viewers will see the score for the number of correctly solved sliders and their individual round earnings based on the Streamer's performance. Viewers can decide to tip the Streamer. This tip is a share of their round earnings between 0 EUR and the round earnings from the Streaming Phase. If the Viewer decides to tip the Streamer, the tip will be deducted from the Viewer's round earnings added to the Streamer's round earnings.

Viewers cannot tip with earnings from previous rounds.
