Phase 3: Results Phase

Instructions

After the Tipping Phase, the Streamer and the Viewers receive a round summary.

Streamer

{{ if player.participant.leaderboard }} The Streamer sees a leaderboard with the top {{ Constants.leaderboard_topn }} tippers for the round.
The Streamer also sees the score for the number of correctly solved sliders, the round earnings based on their performance, the total amount of tips received and the total round earnings including tips. {{ else }} The Streamer sees the number of correctly solved sliders, the round earnings based on their performance, the total amount of tips received and their round earnings including tips. {{ endif }}

Viewer

{{ if player.participant.leaderboard }} Viewers see a leaderboard with the top {{ Constants.leaderboard_topn }} tippers for the round.
Viewers also see the score for the number of correctly solved sliders, the round earnings based on the Streamer's performance, how much they tipped individually and the remaining earnings after tip. {{ else }} Viewers see the number of correctly solved sliders, their round earnings based on the Streamer’s performance, how much they tipped and their remaining earnings after tip. {{ endif }}

After the Results Phase, a new round begins for a total of 10 rounds. At the end of Part 4, 5 rounds are drawn randomly and your payout for this experiment will equal the earnings from those rounds and an {{show_up_fee}} show-up fee.
