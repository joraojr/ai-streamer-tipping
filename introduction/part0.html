The following instructions are the same for all participants. Please read these instructions carefully, as they will explain how you will earn money and how your earnings will depend on the choices that you make.

Please make sure to switch-off and stow away your phones and other electronic devices. If you do not follow these rules, we may have to exclude you from any payments.

In this experiment, you will earn money according to the rules outlined in the following pages. The exact amount will depend on your own decisions and on the decisions of other participants. Your identity will not be revealed to other participants and theirs not to you: in this sense, your participation is anonymous.
