Phase 1: Streaming Phase

Instructions

At the beginning of each round the Streamer performs a slider task, where they need to solve as many sliders as possible within the time-limit. To solve a slider, the Streamer must use the mouse to drag the blue circle to the middle of each slider until it turns green. The blue circle MUST TURN GREEN in order for the slider to count. If the circle remains blue, the slider does not count.

Streamer

For each round, the Streamer has 45 seconds to solve up to 48 sliders. The number of solved sliders are displayed as the Current Score at the top of the screen.

Viewers

Viewers are not able to solve the sliders but instead shown a live-stream of the Streamer's performance on their screens. The Streamer and the Viewers cannot communicate.
